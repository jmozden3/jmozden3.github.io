About Me

I enjoy being the liaison between technical and non-technical stakeholders. I started my career in a non-technical role and have focused on upskilling technically ever since, which unqiquely positions me to help bridge the gap between these two worlds.

My Career

I began my career at InContext Solutions, a retail analytics company. At ICS, I built a new team to develop internal and external products such as automated project outputs and data visualizations. At Deloitte, I consulted clients in the banking and capital markets space on their technical strategy and data analytics needs. At Microsoft, I worked on the Data & AI team, where I helped clients build AI solutions using Azure's data and AI services. At every step in my career, I focus on bringing strong communication skills, grit, and positivity to my work.

Brief Background

I was born in New York City and was raised in the suburbs of Chicago. My undergraduate degree is from Indiana University (Kelley School of Business), where I studied Business Analytics and Professional Sales. Outside of school and work, I'm a group fitness instructor. I think I want to open my own gym one day.
