On Learning in Public

August 2025

There's something powerful about sharing your learning journey openly. When you write about what you're figuring out, you're not just documenting your own progress—you're creating breadcrumbs for others walking similar paths.

I've been thinking about this as I build this website. Every post, every project shared is a small act of vulnerability that can help someone else.
